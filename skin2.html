Our Returns Policy

In accordance with the Distance Selling legislation, we will accept goods returned within 14 days from date of delivery, so long as they are returned in an unused, new condition with the original packaging & the original invoice. We must be notified of intention to return product. Please note, it’s the customer’s responsibility to ensure the goods are returned safely to us and the customer must pay postage costs , and we in turn will pay for your items (in the case of an exchange )to be resent to you.

Goods must be in their original packaging with any seals in tact and in a saleable condition. Please enclose the original receipt with the parcel. Goods must be returned within 14 days of receipt for exchange or refund. Re-package the parcel using strong packaging and ensure it’s sealed securely. Take the parcel to your local Post Office and request a proof of postage. Without this, you will be unable to make a claim, should the parcel go missing. For returns, we will refund the original bank card charged only. (Please note that refunds may not show up on your bank statement immediately).

In the unlikely event that you receive the wrong product please notify us immediately. Please ensure the goods are in their original packaging and we will arrange a pickup with the courier. Once the pickup has occurred we will then arrange to have the correct product sent out to you.

Delivery Information

Delivery of your products will be free of charge to Ireland when you spend €50 or more. Otherwise delivery costs will be €5.00 (€4.06 ex. VAT) to Ireland. All delivery charges are subject to change and delivery is subject to credit card authorisation and stock availability. We aim to deliver your order within 1-2 working days. However, this cannot be guaranteed so we ask that you allow up to 5 working days. Delivery times are based on orders placed between Monday – Friday (excluding bank holidays).

Stock levels may vary from the time you place your order to the time it is processed. If the item is out of stock by the time we process your order you will receive an email stating this and a refund will be given if an alternative product is not preferred. If your order cannot be delivered within 5 working days due to a delay in new stock from our supplier, you will be notified by email.


*Important Notice: For your convenience DPD offer their Parcel Wizard service which notifies you of your delivery details. It also allows you change your delivery time if you will not be at home. If you are not at home at the requested time of your delivery and you have not notified DPD through your “Mange my Delivery” service, the driver can leave your parcel with a neighbour. If you make any pre-arrangements with the driver to leave your package in a safe place NaturalHealingsSkincare cannot accept responsibility for loss or damage.
