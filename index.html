Our Returns Policy

In accordance with the Distance Selling legislation, we will accept goods returned within 14 days from date of delivery, so long as they are returned in an unused, new condition with the original packaging & the original invoice. We must be notified of intention to return product. Please note, it’s the customer’s responsibility to ensure the goods are returned safely to us and the customer must pay postage costs , and we in turn will pay for your items (in the case of an exchange )to be resent to you.

Goods must be in their original packaging with any seals in tact and in a saleable condition. Please enclose the original receipt with the parcel. Goods must be returned within 14 days of receipt for exchange or refund. Re-package the parcel using strong packaging and ensure it’s sealed securely. Take the parcel to your local Post Office and request a proof of postage. Without this, you will be unable to make a claim, should the parcel go missing. For returns, we will refund the original bank card charged only. (Please note that refunds may not show up on your bank statement immediately).

In the unlikely event that you receive the wrong product please notify us immediately. Please ensure the goods are in their original packaging and we will arrange a pickup with the courier. Once the pickup has occurred we will then arrange to have the correct product sent out to you.
